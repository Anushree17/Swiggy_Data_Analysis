# Swiggy_Data_Analysis
<p>This data analysis project focuses on evaluating the performance and trends of restaurants listed on Swiggy.The goal is to derive actionable insights that will assist in making informed business decisions.The analysis will encompass various metrics, including customer ratings, order volume, pricing strategies, and menu diversity. By leveraging this data, we aim to identify key areas for improvement, optimize operational efficiency, and enhance customer satisfaction.</p>

<h2>Key Business Insights</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Rohini had over 250 number of restaurants</li>
  <li>Mumbai was the most expensive in terms of average price of food with price over 350 INR</li>
  <li>Most Restaurants had prices in the range of 300 and 500</li>
  <li>Most restaurants took an average of 40 to50 minutes of Delivery Time</li>
  <li>There was no significant relationship between food price and average rating</li>
  <li>Hyderabad's most popular cuisine was south indian while that of Delhi was north indian</li>
  <li>Bangalore had the maximum average rating of over 4.2</li>
  <li>Only 3.7% of the restaurants listed rated above an average of 4.5</li>
  <li>Kolkata had the highest mean delivery time</li>
  <li>Kolkata's lower average rating is not because of high mean delivey time</li>
  <li>There were roughly 80 restaurants in Kolkata having a delivery time of greater than 67 minutes </li>
  <li>In Bangalore,most of the restaurants served desserts</li>
  <li>In Bangalorr,Vasanth Nagar had the maximum price which was 2500 INR</li>
  <li>In Bangalore,most restaurants have price ranges between 200 and 300</li>
  <li>Bangalore does not exceed delibery time of 60 minutes for maximum number of restaurants</li>
  <li>Koramangala in Bangalore had the highest delivery time of 78 minutes</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Conclusion</h2>
  <p>Overall, the analysis highlighted regional culinary preferences, pricing strategies, and delivery dynamics across major Indian cities, providing valuable insights for restaurant owners and food delivery platforms to tailor their services and offerings for improved customer satisfaction.</p>
